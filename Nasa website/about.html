About Our Team

Who We Are

We are a diverse and passionate group of students from Kazakhstan and the Philippines, united in our commitment to address climate change and promote gender equality in science. As students from the prestigious 125 High School in Kazakhstan, we bring together our unique perspectives and shared vision for a sustainable future.

Our team is driven by the belief that the challenges of climate change require global cooperation and diverse voices. By combining the strengths of our Kazakh and Filipino backgrounds, we aim to bring fresh insights and innovative solutions to the forefront of climate science.

Our Mission

As inspiring and talented high school students, we are dedicated to raising awareness about climate change and promoting the role of women in science. We believe that by combining our diverse backgrounds, relentless work ethic, and passion for equality, we can contribute significantly to the global effort against climate change.

Our project, the Climate Science Explorer, is a testament to our commitment to education, innovation, and action in the face of environmental challenges. We invite you to join us on this journey towards a more sustainable and equitable world.
